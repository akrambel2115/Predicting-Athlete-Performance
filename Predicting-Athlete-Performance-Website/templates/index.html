About PerfoMax

PerfoMax is a cutting-edge athlete performance prediction platform that utilizes search algorithms and constraint satisfaction problems (CSPs) to optimize training routines and improve athletic performance.

Our system analyzes historical performance data, calculates injury risks, and generates personalized training recommendations to help athletes reach their full potential while minimizing the risk of injuries.
